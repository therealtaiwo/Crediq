Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

CrediQ is a JUPEB CBT practice platform built for Nigerian students. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect, what we don't, and how we use your information.

What We Collect

Account information: Your name and email address when you sign up.

Exam data: Your practice session results, scores, and wrong topics. This is used to calculate your readiness score and identify weak areas.

Usage data: How many questions you answer per day, your study streak, and which subjects you practice. This powers the personalisation features.

Payment data: If you upgrade to Premium, Paystack processes your payment. We only receive a transaction reference — we never see or store your bank details or card information.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your phone number, location, contacts, or any data unrelated to your JUPEB preparation. We do not run ads. We do not sell your data to anyone — ever.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to make CrediQ work for you — tracking your progress, showing your readiness score, and identifying topics you need to practice more. We do not use your data for marketing or share it with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored on Google's servers. See Firebase's privacy policy.

Paystack: We use Paystack to process Premium payments. See Paystack's privacy policy.

Data Security

Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. Database access rules ensure you can only read and write your own data. Premium status can only be granted by a verified Paystack payment — it cannot be self-granted.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You can also request a copy of your data.
